BMW M3 CS – PRODUCT HIGHLIGHTS

High-Performance M TwinPower Turbo 6-cylinder petrol engine

The M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder petrol engine with 551 hp features superior power delivery and the striking characteristic M engine sound.

TCS specific driving dynamics

From specific springs, anti-roll bars, damping elements and control arms to traction control: The BMW M3 CS is made for the race track.

Reduced weight

For more agile driving, plastic components reinforced with carbon fibre (CFRP) and intelligent lightweight construction reduce weight by approx. 20 kg compared to the BMW M3 Competition with M xDrive.

DRIVER'S COCKPIT & TECHNOLOGIES

M specific instruments & operating concept, digital services and driver assistance systems of the BMW 3 Series Sedan M Automobiles.

M Drive

With M Drive you can configure your personal driving profiles with your own transmission shifting settings, engine, damper, steering and braking characteristics. Use the red M1/M2 buttons on your M leather steering wheel to activate your M Drive configurations.

M specific instrument cluster

The M specific instrument cluster offers a particularly precise and focused display of driving data in the SPORT and TRACK modes.

BMW Live Cockpit Professional

The BMW Live Cockpit Professional with curved display expands BMW Live Cockpit Plus with a practical, full-colour head-up display.

BMW M PERFORMANCE PARTS

M Performance rear winglet, carbon fibre

The M Performance rear winglets from carbon fibre broaden the vehicle rear, enhancing its sporty, exclusive look.

M Performance rear wing, carbon fibre.

The M Performance rear spoiler with aerodynamic cut-out is made from 100 percent high-quality carbon fibre and creates a distinctive motorsport look at the rear of the vehicle.

M Performance side skirt insert, carbon fibre.
